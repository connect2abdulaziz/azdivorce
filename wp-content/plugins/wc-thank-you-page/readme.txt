== Description ==

ThankRedirect empowers you to take full control over the WooCommerce thank you page. Instead of a generic confirmation, you can create a fully customized experience that delights customers and drives business growth.

With ThankRedirect, you can redirect customers to unique pages based on the products they buy, their user role, the payment method they use, and much more. It's the ultimate tool for personalizing the final step of the customer journey.

### 🚀 Why a Custom Thank You Page is a Game-Changer

The thank you page is one of the most valuable pages in your store. It's where you have your customer's full attention. A strategic thank you page can:

*   **📈 Boost Sales:** Upsell related products, offer exclusive discounts, and encourage repeat purchases.
*   **🤝 Build Loyalty:** Share personalized messages, welcome videos, or helpful resources to make customers feel valued.
*   **💬 Gather Feedback:** Include surveys or review requests to gain valuable insights.
*   **📢 Grow Your Audience:** Encourage social sharing or subscriptions to your newsletter.
*   **📊 Improve Analytics:** Get accurate conversion data with seamless analytics integration.

### ✨ Core Features (Free)

*   **Global Redirect:** Set a default custom thank you page for all purchases.
*   **Per-Product Redirect:** Create unique thank you pages for specific products.
*   **Category-Based Redirect:** Redirect customers based on the product categories in their order.
*   **Page Builder Friendly:** Works seamlessly with Elementor, Gutenberg, and other popular page builders.
*   **Pre-designed Templates:** Get started quickly with our library of professional Elementor and Gutenberg templates.
*   **Dynamic Shortcodes:** Easily display order details, customer information, and more with our simple shortcodes.

### 🛠️ Shortcodes for Easy Customization

Easily display dynamic information on your thank you pages.

**Free Shortcodes:**
*   `[wpkin_order_number]` - The order number.
*   `[wpkin_transaction_id]` - The payment transaction ID.
*   `[wpkin_order_status]` - The current status of the order.
*   `[wpkin_order_created_date]` - The date the order was placed.
*   `[wpkin_customer_name]` - The customer's full name.

**Pro Shortcodes:**
*   `[wpkin_order_total]` - The total order amount.
*   `[wpkin_tax]` / `[wpkin_shipping]` - Tax and shipping costs.
*   `[wpkin_coupon]` - Any coupon codes used.
*   `[wpkin_first_name]` / `[wpkin_customer_email]` - Customer's first name and email.
*   `[wpkin_billing_address]` / `[wpkin_shipping_address]` - Full billing and shipping addresses.
*   `[wpkin_items]` - A list of products purchased.
*   `[wpkin_downloads]` - Download links for digital products.
*   `[wpkin_payment_method]` / `[wpkin_shipping_method]` - Payment and shipping methods.
*   ...and more!

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/wc-thank-you-page` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress.
3. Go to **ThankRedirect** from your WordPress dashboard to configure your settings.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Will this work with my theme? =

Yes! ThankRedirect works with any WordPress theme that supports WooCommerce.

= Can I use different thank you pages for different products? =

Absolutely! The free version lets you set redirects per product, and the Pro version adds even more powerful rules.

= How do I display order information on my thank you page? =

Simply use our built-in shortcodes like `[wpkin_order_number]` to display order details.

= Will this slow down my website? =

No. ThankRedirect is lightweight and optimized for performance. The redirects happen quickly and won't impact your site's speed.

= Can I use this with page builders like Elementor? =

Yes, it works perfectly with Elementor, Gutenberg, and other major page builders.

= Does this work with WPML or other multilingual plugins? =

Yes, ThankRedirect is compatible with WPML and other popular multilingual plugins.

= Can I track conversions with Google Analytics or Facebook Pixel? =

Yes, the Pro version provides robust integration with Google Analytics and Facebook Pixel for accurate conversion tracking.

= What happens if something goes wrong with the redirect? =

If a redirect rule isn't met, customers will simply see the default WooCommerce thank you page. No orders will be affected.
